Deviled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs filled with creamy egg yoke and topped of with your favourite seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Cook the eggs until they are hard boiled.
  2. Slice the eggs in half and remove the yolks.
  3. Mash the yolk together with the mayonnaise and seasonings until they are creamy.
  4. Fill the eggs with the yolk mixture.
  5. Garnish the eggs with paprika and/or add other seasonings/herbs.
