Lasagne

Description

Tasty and flavorful lasagne cooked with love.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta Cook the pasta in a bowl of salted water.
  2. Cook the italian sausages and ground beef with some sliced garlic and onions.
  3. Stir the cheese mixture in a bowl.
  4. Layer the meat and cheese mixture together with lasagne noodles in a form.
  5. Bake the mixture until brown and bubbly.
