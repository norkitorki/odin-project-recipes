Lemon Pound Cake

Description

Delicious neutral cake with distinct lemon flavor.

Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda

Steps

  1. Zest and juice lemons.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Cream butter & sugar with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  4. Add flour and sour cream and beat on low speed until combined.
  5. Spread batter into a prepared pan.
  6. Bake until brown.
  7. Cool off the cake and add the glazing.
